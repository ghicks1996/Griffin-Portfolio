The Best Movies According to Griffin

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time:

Children of Men

A gripping science fiction film about the downfall of society after women lose the ability to concieve children.

Paris Texas

A really beautiful and deeply personal film about isolation, regret and lost love. Includes an incredible lead performance by Harry Dean Stanton.

Chinatown

My favorite Noir/Detective film starring my favorite actor Jack Nicholson, who plays a private-eye tasked with finding a woman's missing husband. This small mystery reveals a much larger conspiracy taking place in the city of L.A.
